04 | Life Processes

Chalo bhai shuru krte hai!

Agar dog ya cow chal rahe hain, ya man chill kar raha hai, toh humein samajh aata hai wo living hain.

Agar wo so rahe hain, toh toh kaise jaanenge? Breathing dekh kr bolte hain, ‘Haan, zinda hain!’ Plant? Wo breathe nahi karte, lekin green hote hain aur photosynthesis karte hain, isliye wo bhi living hote hain! Aur virus? Ye living tab hote hain jab ye kisi cell ke andar jaate hain aur multiply karte hain.

Life Processes

Life Process ka matlab kya hai? Shabd ko todte hain — ‘Life’ matlab living aur ‘Processes’ matlab prakriya. Matlab, living organisms ke andar jo processes hote hai. Beta, wo process jo zindagi jeene ke liye zaroori ho, essential ho, jo hamari zindagi ko maintain kare, sustain kare – un processes ko hum life processes bolte hain.

Jaise dost ke saath baith ke panchayat karna – ye essential nahi hai beta, ye masti hai.

✕

Lekin khana khana, saans lena, – yeh sab essential hai. Inke bina survival hi possible nahi hai.

✓
❝

The basic and essential functions or processes performed by living organisms to maintain and sustain their life.

❞

Iss chapter mein humein 4 life processes detail mein padhne hain:

Nutrition

The process of obtaining and utilisation of food.

Respiration

The process of breaking down food to obtain energy.

Transportation

The process of transfer of substances from one part of the body to other parts.

Excretion

The process of removal of waste materials produced in the cells of the body.
